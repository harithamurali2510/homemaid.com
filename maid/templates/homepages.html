What We Do?

A common problem faced by numerous urban house-holds is the difficulty in finding a suitable house keeper. When attempting to find such a housekeeper, the main problem they usually encounter is in finding a maid who meets all their required criteria. These details are done by third party private agencies, which are not affiliated to any government organisation and charge exorbitant amount of money in the form of commission. To combat this, our website aims to act as a connecting bridge between urban and rural households, by bringing suitable house help as well as provide a consistent source of income to rural households.

How it works?

In the maid login page, respective e-Akshaya centres can login and add members when they are approached. The main credentials of the aforesaid maid are entered. The client page, after login, shows the closest results, filtered according to the type of chores required as well as the monthly salary of the maid. Then a suitable maid is found, the client can view all their details and contact him/her for further clarification. After every term of employment, the maid has to notify the e-Akshaya centre, so that his/her profile can again be deemed as available.
